After your client makes payments, you receive your percentage payments within or after 24hrs to you local bank account.

Yes. Send us a message via any of our provided options to request for an update your payment information.

Is this a secure site for Payment and purchases?

Absolutely! We work with top payment companies which guarantees your safety and security. All billing information is stored on our payment processing partner.

How would my customer make payments?

Your customers make payment through your private link that will be given to you within 24hrs after registration.

How long are your contracts?

Currently, we only offer monthly subscription. You can upgrade or cancel your monthly account at any time with no further obligation.

Can I request refund?

Unfortunately, not. We do not issue full or partial refunds for our promo Package but, for our standard packages, Partial refunds are possible.

Can my percentage increase overtime?

We will notify you on any increase or decrease in the percentage payment plan.

What happens if my customer pays directly to you without using my link?

We are always willing to look into any issue provided there are valid proofs to any claim

Do you start without payments?

We do not take up any offer without a valid payment. However, we start our development for an agreed upfront payment for only our standard packages.

Do I get bonuses for introducing new affiliates?

Currently not! In future we look forward to implementing compensations. However, we will notify you on any changes.
